VIGILINK-SOS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Vigilink-SOS ("the Application") is a personal safety application developed by Vigilink. We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

2. Data Collected

The Application collects the following data:

  • Name and phone number — to identify your account and send SOS alerts
  • Emergency contacts — names and phone numbers of people to contact in case of emergency
  • GPS location — only at the time of an SOS alert, to locate and transmit your position to your emergency contacts
  • Medical profile (optional) — blood type, allergies, medications, medical conditions, voluntarily entered by the user
  • Security journal (optional) — notes and photos added by the user

3. Use of Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Send emergency SMS to your contacts when an SOS is triggered
  • Display your GPS location in the emergency message
  • Generate your emergency medical QR code
  • Synchronize your profile across your devices (if you enable cloud sync)
  • Provide the Guardian AI chat service (messages are not retained)

4. Data Storage

  • Locally — Most of your data (contacts, settings, journal) is stored only on your device via the browser's local storage (localStorage)
  • On server — If you enable cloud synchronization, your encrypted profile is stored on our secure PostgreSQL server. Your password is never transmitted or stored — only a derived token (SHA-256 hash) is used for authentication
  • SMS via Twilio — SOS messages are sent through the Twilio service. We do not retain SMS logs beyond the session

5. Data Sharing

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial or advertising purposes.

Your data is shared only with:

  • Twilio — for sending emergency SMS (phone numbers and message content only)
  • Stripe — for payment processing (no credit card data passes through our servers)
  • OpenAI — for Guardian AI chat (conversation messages only, without personally identifiable data)

6. Device Permissions

  • Geolocation — to include your position in SOS alerts
  • Notifications — for timer alerts and confirmations
  • Vibration — for haptic feedback during alerts
  • Motion sensors — for fall and shake detection (Platinum only)

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • Encrypted communications via HTTPS
  • Derived token authentication (SHA-256) — your password never leaves your device in clear text
  • No payment data stored on our servers
  • Temporary emergency profiles (deleted after 24 hours)

8. User Rights

You can at any time:

  • Delete your account and all your local data via the application settings
  • Request deletion of your cloud data by contacting us
  • Modify or remove your emergency contacts and medical information
  • Revoke device permissions via your phone settings

9. Children

The Application is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors under 13.

10. Changes

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
